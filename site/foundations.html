Motion

Per-brand easing + duration tokens carry the brand's feel. Components read --npt-ease-* / --npt-dur-*; reduced-motion is honoured everywhere.

Standard

--npt-ease-standard · --npt-dur-standard — most transitions.

Emphasized

--npt-ease-emphasized · --npt-dur-slow — entrances & hero moments.

Spring

--npt-ease-spring — selection overshoot (M3 Expressive).

Accessibility

  • WCAG AA contrast on text (4.5:1) and large/UI (3:1) — baked into the role pairs.
  • Visible focus (:focus-visible), full keyboard nav, ≥48dp targets.
  • RTL by birth — logical properties only; Arabic faces swap per brand.
  • Reduced motion honoured via prefers-reduced-motion.
