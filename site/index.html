One core. Every bank.

Neptune Odyssey is a white-label banking design system by Neptune.Fintech — one Material 3 foundation that any bank re-skins (colour, shape, type, motion, expression) across light/dark and LTR/RTL. Pick a theme, copy its brandprint, and get the identical result on Flutter, Web, Svelte, Vue and React.
